# Project Title
PharmaChain: An Integrated Optimization and Risk Analytics Platform for Pharmaceutical Supply Chains

# Abstract
We are building an integrated optimization and risk analytics platform designed to solve the complex challenges of centralized pharmaceutical warehousing. Our solution models and evaluates the supply chain network to balance transportation costs, delivery times, and strict cold chain capacity constraints. We are developing a robust pipeline that leverages mathematical optimization to assign optimal routing, alongside stochastic simulation to test the network against probabilistic demand surges and infrastructure failures. Furthermore, we are integrating a predictive model to proactively identify vulnerability bottlenecks before they disrupt regional drug distribution. The entire system converges into an interactive dashboard that provides decision makers with a comprehensive view of geospatial risks, capacity utilization, and strategic infrastructure upgrade pathways. By combining operations research with predictive analytics, this tool empowers pharmaceutical distributors to transition from reactive crisis management to proactive supply chain resilience. The platform ultimately ensures that critical medical supplies reach their destinations efficiently and reliably, minimizing the likelihood of shortages under both normal and disrupted operating conditions.
